About

Ph.D. student in Software Engineering at UESTC, working at the Intelligent Computing and Data Mining Lab. My research focuses on biometric template protection — designing cancelable, privacy-preserving representations of fingerprints, palmprints, and multimodal biometrics using deep attention architectures and cryptographic hashing. I also work on video anomaly detection and adversarial machine learning, bridging theoretical security guarantees with practical deep learning systems.
